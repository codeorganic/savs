Show & Sale

    Show & Sale

    Where?

    The Seattle African Violet Society’s annual Show and Sale, held jointly with the Puget Sound Gesneriad Society, will take place at the Conservatory in Volunteer Park, Seattle, 1400 E. Galer Street, Saturday, April 25, 2025 from 10am to 3pm.

    Cost

    The show can be viewed at the main greenhouse which will require a small entry fee. Sales will take place in the greenhouse behind the main building, to which admission is FREE.
